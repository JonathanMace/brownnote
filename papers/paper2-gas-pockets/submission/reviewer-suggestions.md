# Suggested reviewers for Paper 2 (JASA)

Suggested reviewers were chosen to cover bubble dynamics, biomedical acoustics, and gastrointestinal mechanotransduction. Before submission, the authors should still screen for conflicts of interest, recent collaborations, and editorial roles.

## 1. Kausik Sarkar
- **Affiliation:** George Washington University, Biomedical Acoustics and Complex Fluids Lab
- **Expertise:** Biomedical acoustics, multiphase flows, microbubble dynamics, ultrasound imaging and drug delivery
- **Why a good fit:** Strong match to the paper's constrained-bubble modelling and acoustics-in-soft-media aspects.
- **Source:** https://www2.seas.gwu.edu/~sarkar/

## 2. Mario L. Fabiilli
- **Affiliation:** University of Michigan Medical School
- **Expertise:** Acoustic droplet vaporisation, bubble nucleation and dynamics, ultrasound-responsive biomaterials
- **Why a good fit:** Relevant expertise in bubble oscillation and acoustic behaviour in soft biological materials.
- **Source:** https://ultrasound.med.umich.edu/principal-investigators/mario-fabiilli/publications

## 3. Philippe Marmottant
- **Affiliation:** University Grenoble Alpes, LIPhy
- **Expertise:** Bubble dynamics in soft and biological matter, nonlinear oscillations, microbubble acoustics
- **Why a good fit:** Particularly well suited to assess whether the paper's constrained-bubble assumptions are physically reasonable in tissue-like media.
- **Source:** http://liphy-annuaire.univ-grenoble-alpes.fr/pages_personnelles/philippe_marmottant/Home.html

## 4. Arthur Beyder
- **Affiliation:** Mayo Clinic, Gastrointestinal Mechanotransduction Laboratory
- **Expertise:** Gastrointestinal mechanotransduction, mechanoelectrical feedback, mechanically evoked signalling in gut tissue
- **Why a good fit:** Ideal reviewer for the mechanotransduction and gut-physiology plausibility of the proposed pathway.
- **Source:** https://www.mayo.edu/research/labs/gastrointestinal-mechanotransduction/research/projects

## 5. Rodger A. Liddle
- **Affiliation:** Duke University
- **Expertise:** Enteroendocrine signalling, gut sensory biology, gastrointestinal mechanosensing and PIEZO-related physiology
- **Why a good fit:** Strong choice for evaluating the biological interpretation linking local wall motion to GI sensory signalling.
