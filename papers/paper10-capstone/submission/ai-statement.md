# Artificial Intelligence Disclosure Statement

**Paper**: Geometry Mediates Excitation, Resonance, and Identifiability in Fluid-Filled Viscoelastic Shells

**Authors**: Jonathan Mace, Brian R. Mace

## AI Tools Used

**GitHub Copilot** (GitHub, Inc.) was used during the preparation of this manuscript for:

- **Code development**: Assistance with implementing analytical models, numerical routines, and parametric sweep scripts in Python.
- **Figure generation**: Assistance with producing publication-quality figures using matplotlib.
- **Manuscript drafting**: Assistance with LaTeX formatting, prose drafting, and reference management.

## Author Responsibility

All mathematical derivations, proofs (Theorems 1–2, Propositions, and Lemma), scientific content, physical interpretations, and conclusions presented in the manuscript were formulated, verified, and approved by the authors. The AI tool was used as a productivity aid; it did not contribute original scientific ideas or independent analysis.

## Computational Validation

All computational results were independently validated by the authors through:

- Comparison with analytical closed-form solutions where available.
- Mesh and mode convergence studies.
- Cross-checking of canonical parameter values against published literature.
- Regression testing via an automated test suite (487 tests).

## Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Royal Society's policy on the use of artificial intelligence in research publications.
