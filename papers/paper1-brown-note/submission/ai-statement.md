# Declaration of Generative AI Use

**Manuscript:** Can infrasound induce abdominal resonance? A viscoelastic
oblate-spheroidal shell model of the brown note hypothesis

**Journal:** Journal of Sound and Vibration

---

## AI tools used

**GitHub Copilot CLI** (powered by large language models) was used during the
preparation of this manuscript in the following capacities:

1. **Code drafting** — Copilot assisted with scaffolding Python modules for the
   analytical model (modal analysis, parametric sweeps, figure generation
   scripts). All equations, numerical implementations, and simulation outputs
   were independently verified by the human authors.

2. **Literature organisation** — Copilot helped collate, format, and
   cross-check bibliographic entries. Every cited reference was read and
   verified for relevance and accuracy by the human authors.

3. **Language editing** — Copilot suggested phrasing improvements and helped
   ensure consistent British English throughout the manuscript. All scientific
   claims, interpretations, and conclusions were written and approved by the
   human authors.

## Human oversight

The authors take full responsibility for the content of this publication. All
theoretical derivations, physical interpretations, parameter choices, numerical
results, and scientific conclusions were formulated, checked, and validated by
the human authors. No AI tool was used to generate or fabricate data, produce
figures without human verification, or draw scientific conclusions.
