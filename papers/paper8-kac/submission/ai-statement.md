# Draft AI disclosure for Paper 8 submission

## Recommended statement

During manuscript preparation, the authors used GitHub Copilot CLI (GitHub,
Inc.) to assist with code drafting, document preparation, and language editing.
All equations, simulations, references, interpretations, and conclusions were
reviewed and verified by the human authors, who take full responsibility for
the content of the manuscript. No generative AI tool was used to create or
alter original research data or figures.

## Placement note

If the target journal requests disclosure of generative-AI assistance, this
statement can be entered into the submission system metadata or adapted for a
brief note in the manuscript. The conservative approach is to disclose
tool-assisted drafting whenever AI support extended beyond copy-editing.

## Policy summary for this package

- AI tools were used for drafting support and language refinement only.
- Human authors reviewed and approved all scientific content.
- AI tools cannot be listed as authors.
- AI tools were not used to fabricate references or modify original data/images.
