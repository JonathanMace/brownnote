# Suggested reviewers for Paper 4

Potential reviewers were selected for expertise spanning bladder biomechanics,
urodynamics, whole-body vibration, soft-tissue shell mechanics, and
fluid--structure interaction in biological systems. All are outside Microsoft
Research and the University of Auckland.

## 1. Margot Damaser

- **Affiliation:** Department of Biomedical Engineering, Lerner Research Institute, Cleveland Clinic; Case Western Reserve University
- **Email:** `damasem@ccf.org`
- **Relevant papers:**
  - *Voiding function in rats with simulated birth trauma and pudendal nerve injury*, **Neurourology and Urodynamics** 24 (2005) 1–5.
  - *Finite element analysis of the mouse bladder wall under distension*, **Journal of Biomechanics** 46 (2013) 1369–1375.
- **Reason:** Damaser brings direct expertise in computational bladder wall mechanics and urodynamic modelling. Her finite-element work on bladder distension is closely related to our fill-dependent shell model and the interplay between geometry and wall stiffening that governs the U-shaped frequency–volume relation.
- **Sources:**
  - Cleveland Clinic profile: <https://www.lerner.ccf.org/bme/damaser/>
  - Google Scholar: <https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8cIr45cAAAAJ&hl=en>

## 2. Raphaël Oriez (Raphaël Bastiaens)

- **Email:** `raphael.oriez@univ-grenoble-alpes.fr`
- **Relevant papers:**
