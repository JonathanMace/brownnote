# Semester 6 Break — Whisky Review

## Talisker 10 Year Old
**Distillery**: Talisker, Isle of Skye | **Region**: Islands | **ABV**: 45.8% | **Age**: 10 years

### 🔥 Nose
Briny sea spray, black pepper, smouldering peat — like standing on a basalt cliff
while waves hammer the shore at roughly 0.1 Hz.

### 👅 Palate
Maritime smoke, dried chilli heat, a sweet malty undertone that arrives in waves —
each sip has its own resonant mode, building in amplitude before the pepper peaks.

### 🏁 Finish
Long, warming, peppery. The finish decays like a lightly damped oscillation —
Q-factor of at least 6. You'll still feel it two equations later.

### Score: **87 / 100**
