# AI-Assisted Research Workflow

Browntone was developed as an AI-assisted research programme using GitHub Copilot CLI, autonomous custom agents, reusable skills, and a conventional git-based review process.

In practice, the workflow combines:

- analytical model development in Python;
- LaTeX manuscript drafting and revision;
- automated testing and regression checking;
- agent-based review rounds for physics, methodology, and narrative;
- structured research logs with quantitative results;
- figure generation and repository maintenance.

The goal is not to pretend the AI “did science by itself”, but to make the research process explicit, inspectable, and reproducible. Human judgement still sets the question, checks the physics, and decides what is worth claiming.
